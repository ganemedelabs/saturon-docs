Welcome to the Saturon recipes! Here you'll find practical examples and guides on how to use various features of the Saturon library effectively.
Explore the different recipes to enhance your understanding and make the most out of Saturon in your projects.

## Available Recipes

- [Register Named Colors](/docs/recipes/register-named-colors): Learn how to add custom named colors to Saturon for easier color management.
- [Register Color Functions](/docs/recipes/register-color-functions/hsi): Discover how to create and register your own color functions to extend Saturon's capabilities.
- [Register Color Spaces](/docs/recipes/register-color-spaces/display-p3-linear): A guide on how to define and register new color spaces within Saturon.
- [Register Plugins](/docs/recipes/register-plugins): Find out how to create and register plugins to enhance Saturon's functionality.
